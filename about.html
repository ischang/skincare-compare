About this website

This wobsite was written in: vanilla Javascript, some Ajax/JQuery, HTML/CSS and Twitter Bootstrap magic. It's hosted on Github pages and all the business logic is done completely client side.

Basically, that means nothing you input will get stored (except by your own local machine/browser) -- there's no server on my end or anything that's going to see what you typed or uploaded.

Why I wrote this

This website began as a simple Python script to make my life easier. I have extremely sensitive skin that breaks out in severe eczema patches when triggered by allergens or heat, so finding products that worked for my skin has always been extremely difficult.

For years, I avoided skincare products and never put on sunscreen or moistuirizer because it was always a gamble of whether my face would blow up and turn scaly. But one day, I thought enough was enough. Through trial and error, loads of research, I started a spreadsheet and started comparing what products worked, and what triggered my eczema.

I wanted a tool that could let me compare the laundry list of ingredients that each product seemed to have, but besides this Cosdna tool I found on Reddit, which didn't work for me, there wasn't anything else.

So I wrote this. The logic behind the scripts I wrote are relatively simple, but it hopefully works in a way to make comparing ingredients simpler. :)
